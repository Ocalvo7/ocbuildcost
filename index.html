What's calculated
EVERY FEE.
ONE PLACE.

We pull all applicable fees so nothing gets missed when you're estimating a project.

Section B
Building permit fee
Tiered by occupancy classification and square footage. Single family through commercial, new construction through tenant improvements.
Section D
Plan review fee
90% of the building permit fee, paid at time of submittal. Includes up to 3 plan reviews — 4th and beyond billed hourly at $190/review.
Section B footnote
MEP add-ons
Plumbing, electrical, and mechanical review each add 10% to the base permit fee when the scope includes that trade.
Section E
State & admin fees
NPDES stormwater (10%), permit issuance ($79), automation ($40), SMIP Strong Motion, and Green Building Standards SB 1473 — all included.
Pages 8–9
Grading permit fees
Separate grading plan check and permit fees calculated by cubic yards of material moved, from under 500 CY to over 100,000 CY.
Section C
Valuation-based fees
For projects not in Sections A or B. Calculated from total project valuation using the ICC Building Valuation Data Table methodology.
Section A
Common permit types
HVAC change-out, re-roof, windows, solar, pools, fences, retaining walls, patio covers, and more — flat-fee permits with exact amounts.
Violation fees
Unpermitted work
Work done without a permit triggers an investigation fee equal to 3× the normal permit fee. We flag this prominently so contractors never miss it.

Important disclaimer

OC Build Cost calculates permit fees from the official City of San Juan Capistrano Schedule of User and Regulatory Fees, effective July 1, 2025. Fees are subject to annual CPI adjustment. Additional fees may apply for planning review, engineering review, and third-party agency fees not included in this calculator. This tool is for estimation purposes only — actual fees are determined by the Building Department at time of permit submittal. Always verify with the City before submitting.

Important Disclaimer

OC Build Cost provides estimates for planning purposes only. Permit fees are calculated from published municipal fee schedules and are subject to change. Material and labor costs reflect current Orange County market data and may vary based on site conditions, supplier pricing, and subcontractor availability. Estimates may carry a ±20–35% margin of error. This tool does not constitute a contractor bid or professional cost estimate. Always verify fees with the relevant Building Department and obtain three or more bids from CSLB-licensed contractors before committing to a project budget.

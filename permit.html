Key Reminders
  • Fees include up to 3 plan reviews. 4th submittal onward: $190 per review.
  • Re-inspection fee (3rd time or more, same issue): $190 each.
  • After-hours inspection: $228/hr, 4-hour minimum.
  • Work done without a permit: investigation fee = permit fee × 3, in addition to all permit fees.
  • Permit extension: $79. Permit reissuance: $79. Temporary Certificate of Occupancy: $570.
  • Solar permits: total fees shall not exceed amounts in CA Government Code 66015(a)(1). City will not collect additional permit processing fees beyond amounts shown.

Fees calculated per City of San Juan Capistrano Schedule of User and Regulatory Fees, effective July 1, 2025. Fees marked "Y'rly CPI" adjust annually with CPI. Additional fees may apply for planning review, engineering review, FEMA flood plain review, and other agency fees. Actual fees are determined by the Building Department at time of permit submittal. Always confirm with the City before submitting.
